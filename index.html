Facts about Cheetahs

  • Cheetahs are the fastest land animal on Earth.
  • Cheetahs have around 2000 spots.
  • Cheetahs use their spotted coat as a disguise.
  • Cheetahs can give birth to 2-8 cubs at a time.
  • Cheetahs don’t roar.
  • Cheetahs have excellent eyesight.
  • Cheetahs only hunt during the day.
  • Cheetahs live up to 10 years in the wild.
  • Can survive up to 10 days without water
  • The population of cheetahs is only around 7000
